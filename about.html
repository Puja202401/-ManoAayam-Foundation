About Us

Quality Education for All

The Indian Forum of Educators, established in 2020, is dedicated to achieving SDG-4 by ensuring equitable access to inclusive, quality education at all levels. Our objective is to foster lifelong learning opportunities for all individuals, working collaboratively with educators, policymakers, and stakeholders to create a more inclusive and equitable education system in India.

About Foundation



Indian Forum of Educators is the body that has been working since 2020 to achieve SDG-4.

Objective:

Ensure equitable access to inclusive, quality education at all levels, fostering lifelong learning opportunities for all individuals.

Key Actions
  • Explore the potential of public-private partnerships (PPPs) to elevate the standard of government schools in India by building Better Schools.
  • - leveraging private sector expertise, increasing accountability, and optimizing resource allocation.
  • - Improve teacher training and support, enhance school infrastructure, and invest in educational resources to enhance the quality of learning.
  • - Foster inclusive and diverse learning environments that accommodate different learning styles, abilities, and cultural backgrounds
  • - Promote education for sustainable development, including environmental literacy, wellness education, emotional literacy, legal literacy and civic engagement, to empower individuals to address global challenges.
