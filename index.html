Welcome to

ManoAayam Foundation

At ManoAayam Foundation we believe in fostering positive change and creating a better world for all. Our foundation is dedicated to promoting four main Sustainable Development Goals [SDG] that we believe are essential for a thriving society:

Read more

Quality Education for All:


Education is the key to unlocking opportunities and fulfilling potential. We advocate for inclusive and accessible education systems that empower individuals of all backgrounds to learn, grow, and succeed

Environmental Sustainability and Conservation:

Protecting our planet is crucial for the well-being of current and future generations. We are committed to promoting environmental sustainability and conservation efforts that preserve natural resources, mitigate climate change, and safeguard biodiversity.

Good Health and Well-being:



Health is the foundation of a fulfilling life. We advocate for policies and initiatives that prioritize physical, mental, and emotional well-being for individuals and communities alike.


Equitable Access to Opportunities and Resources:

We strive to break down barriers and ensure that everyone has fair and equal access to opportunities and resources necessary for personal and professional development.



About Us

Quality Education for All

The Indian Forum of Educators, established in 2020, is dedicated to achieving SDG-4 by ensuring equitable access to inclusive, quality education at all levels. Our objective is to foster lifelong learning opportunities for all individuals, working collaboratively with educators, policymakers, and stakeholders to create a more inclusive and equitable education system in India.
